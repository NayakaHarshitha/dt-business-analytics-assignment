# Research Methodology

## Research Sources Used
- Company websites
- LinkedIn
- BSE filings
- MCA/Tofler
- Industry news articles
- Google Search
- Export and certification databases

## Research Process
1. Selected Hyderabad as the target city
2. Selected specialty biotech and specialty chemical companies
3. Verified whether companies were manufacturers
4. Checked technical leadership and founder background
5. Evaluated differentiation using patents, certifications, and R&D signals
6. Verified growth signals using hiring, facility expansion, certifications, and news updates
7. Assigned Federer scores based on the provided framework

## AI Usage and Verification
AI tools were used for:
- Initial company discovery
- Structuring analysis
- Summarization support

Verification methods:
- Cross-checking company claims on official websites
- Comparing financial information with public filings
- Validating certifications and facility information from external sources
