# 1000 Company Scaling Proposal

## Goal
Build a verified list of 1000 ICP-qualified specialty manufacturing companies within one month.

## Sources Used
- DSIR recognized R&D company lists
- Industry expos and exhibitor lists
- BSE SME and NSE Emerge companies
- MCA filings
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Industry association directories

## Automation Strategy
- Use AI tools for first-level classification
- Use scraping tools to collect website and company information
- Use structured scoring based on DeepThought’s Federer framework

## Qualification Process
1. Identify manufacturing companies
2. Verify India-based operations
3. Check technical leadership background
4. Verify differentiation through certifications, patents, or R&D
5. Check growth signals such as hiring, facility expansion, and financial growth
6. Assign Federer scores

## Quality Control
- Cross-verification using company websites and public filings
- Manual review of borderline companies
- Removal of service companies and non-manufacturers

## Weekly Execution Plan

### Week 1
Build initial company database and collect sources.

### Week 2
Scrape company data and perform AI-assisted classification.

### Week 3
Perform verification and quality checks.

### Week 4
Finalize 1000 qualified companies and prepare outreach-ready database.

## Conclusion
The combination of AI-assisted research, structured verification, and manual quality control can efficiently generate a high-quality ICP-qualified company database at scale.
